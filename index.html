Transportation made simple!

At AUM we are driven to give students the best experience possible. Getting around Montgomery safely and affordably is part of that great experience. Warhawk Transit makes regular stops in a city that has so much to offer! During the fall and spring semester, the transit runs:

  • Monday-Thursday starting at 8:30 am and ending at 10:30 pm
  • Friday from 7:30 am until 10:30 pm
  • Saturday from 11:00 am until 10:30 pm
  • Sunday from 12:15 pm to 8:30 pm
During the summer, the transit runs on a more limited schedule. Campus pick up and drop off is located in front of the Nest adjacent to Warhawk Hall (near the traffic circle at the main entrance of campus).
